SUNDARBAN

- the Mangrove Forest in bangladesh

The sundarbans is the largest mangrove forest in the world.It has been declared the 52nd world haritage site in the world.The sundarbans are in the southwest part of bangladesh in the district of grater khulna.Heres the picture-


The sundarbans is a part of the worlds largest delta formed by the rivers gangs, brahmaputra,and meghna on the bay of bangal.


The sundarbans are famous for their uniqe ecosystem and rich wildlife habitat. It is the natural habitat of the world famous Royal bangal tiger,spotted Deer,Crocodile,jungle fowl,wild boar,lizards,and many more.
